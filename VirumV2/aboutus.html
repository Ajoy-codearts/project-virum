about us

VRium Robotics & VR Amusement Park is the first of its kind in Alabama. Created with the idea behind how technology, science, robotics, entertainment, and culture and how they work together to create fun and immersive experiences.

Whether you are a VR pro or have never tried VR, our VRium Park team will make sure you have the best time!

Electrosa Group, an Ontario based corporation established in 2006, is the parent company of VRium Robotics & VR Amusement Park. Electrosa companies engage in innovation, engineering, distribution and service of technology products and solutions in a variety of industries such as, Aerospace and Defense, Renewable Energy, Industrial Electronic Repair, IT, Surveillance and Alarm, Virtual Reality, and more.
